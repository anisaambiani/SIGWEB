Haechan

Lee Dong-hyuck, known by his stage name Haechan, is a South Korean singer and dancer born in Seoul on June 6, 2000 (he later moved to Jeju Island with his family before becoming a trainee). He is the main vocalist and the lead dancer of NCT 127 and NCT Dream.

He attended the School of Performing Arts (SOPA), and has a younger sister and two younger brothers.
